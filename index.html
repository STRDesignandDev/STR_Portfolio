ABOUT ME

Bio

I consider myself an exceptional problem solver, I'm driven by complexity and strive to deliver simplicity. As a UX/UI designer, who is also an experienced visual designer and artist, I enjoy bringing human centered design concepts to life through my work.

My approach to web and app design is holistic, I utilize excellent communication skills, combined with a strong knowledge of code, to bridge the gap between business needs and technology.

Background

I've worked in a large variety of roles for many different industry types. I have contributed my skills through graphic design, UX/UI design, and front end web development. I love collaborating with like minded individuals on a team, as well as working independently.

I have a unique perspective and style to approaching design solutions. My experience spans the gammet from visual design and marketing, to complex design systems and front end coding.
